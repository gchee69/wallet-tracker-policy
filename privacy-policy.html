Privacy Policy for Wallet Tracker

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Wallet Tracker ("the App") is a personal expense tracking app developed for individual use. This policy explains what information is and isn't collected when you use the App.

1. Data You Enter

All expense entries you create (item, amount, category, date, notes, etc.) are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to us, stored on any server, or shared with any third party. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, this information is permanently deleted.

2. Advertising

The App displays ads served through Google AdMob. To provide and personalize ads, Google AdMob may collect and process:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • App interaction data (ad clicks and impressions)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy. We do not control how Google uses this data beyond ad delivery. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads, on most Android devices).

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require account creation, and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other directly identifying personal information.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the contact details listed on the Wallet Tracker Google Play Store listing.
